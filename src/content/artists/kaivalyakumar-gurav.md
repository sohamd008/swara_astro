Pt. Kaivalyakumar Gurav is a leading vocalist of the Kirana Gharana, renowned for his high-pitch singing and effortless command over the 'saptaks' (octaves). Born into a musical family, he received his rigorous training from his father and guru, Pt. Sangameshwar Gurav, a doyen of the Kirana Gharana.

His singing style is characterized by a rich, resonant voice, intricate 'taans', and an emotive delivery that captures the soul of the raga. He has carved a niche for himself with his ability to blend the traditional nuances of Kirana Gharana with his own aesthetic innovations, making his performances both intellectually stimulating and emotionally moving.

Pt. Kaivalyakumar has performed extensively across India and abroad, earning accolades for his mastery over both Khayal and Thumri. He is also a dedicated guru, imparting his knowledge to the next generation of classical musicians.
